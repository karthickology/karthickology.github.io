Content Creation Checklist

Pre-Writing Requirements

  • Keyword research completed with search intent identified
  • Competitor analysis for content gaps and opportunities
  • Target audience persona and pain points defined
  • Content objective and call-to-action determined
  • Outline created with logical flow and structure

Writing & Optimization

  • Title includes primary keyword (near beginning)
  • Meta description contains keyword and value proposition
  • URL is clean, descriptive, and includes keyword
  • Headings (H1-H3) used properly with keyword variations
  • Content addresses user intent comprehensively
  • Images optimized with descriptive alt text
  • Internal links to relevant content included
  • External links to authoritative sources included
  • Content passes readability tests (grade level appropriate)

E-A-T & Content Quality Standards

Expertise, Authoritativeness, Trustworthiness

Google's E-A-T guidelines emphasize the importance of content created by experts, demonstrated authority in the field, and trustworthy information presentation.

E-A-T Component Implementation Content Examples Verification
Expertise Author credentials, Experience citations Tutorials by practitioners, Case studies Author bios, Certifications, Portfolio
Authoritativeness Backlinks from authoritative sites, Industry recognition Research papers, Industry reports Backlink profile, Industry mentions
Trustworthiness Transparency, Accuracy, Security Product reviews with testing, Medical advice with citations HTTPS, Clear privacy policy, Contact information

Mobile-First Content Optimization

Mobile SEO Priorities

With mobile-first indexing, ensure content is fully accessible, readable, and functional on mobile devices with fast load times and responsive design.

Mobile Content Guidelines

  • Content width adapted for mobile screens (max 100% width)
  • Font size minimum 16px for body text on mobile
  • Touch targets (buttons, links) at least 44x44 pixels
  • Images responsive with proper compression for mobile
  • Content hierarchy clear without horizontal scrolling
  • Page load time under 3 seconds on mobile
  • Interstitial pop-ups minimized for better UX
